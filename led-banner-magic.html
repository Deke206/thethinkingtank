Animated LED information banner demonstration using ShyneTyme colors.

Same colors. More personality.

This is the breadcrumb area turned into a real LED display.

The banner automatically rotates through four effects. Click or press Enter on it to jump to the next one. The text is drawn as individual glowing pixels instead of ordinary browser lettering.

Rainbow chaseThe message scrolls while cyan, blue, violet, pink, amber and mint chase through the letters.
Center-out bloomThe message builds outward from its center with bright moving edge sparks.
Electric scannerA bright amber and cyan scan bar searches through the full message.
Type-on sparkleThe message appears from left to right with a flickering neon cursor.
