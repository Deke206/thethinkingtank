# Privacy Notice

Effective date: July 11, 2026

This website may collect information that a visitor voluntarily submits through an installation-request or contact form, including name, contact information, vehicle type, project description, photographs, preferred appointment details, and requested lighting features.

Information submitted through the website is intended only for responding to inquiries, preparing estimates, scheduling work, documenting approved projects, and communicating about requested services.

Customer information will not be intentionally published in this public repository. Form submissions and private customer records should be stored outside GitHub in an access-controlled service.

Third-party services such as Google Forms, GitHub Pages, embedded media, analytics, mapping, payment, or scheduling tools may apply their own privacy terms.

To request correction or deletion of submitted information, use the contact method displayed on the published website.
